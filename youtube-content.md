# YouTube Content AI Prompt Pack

---

## 1. Video Hook/Opening

**Input:** [Video topic, target audience, main value proposition]

**Prompt:**
Write a 15-30 second video hook that:
- Opens with a bold statement or question addressing [audience]'s biggest pain point
- Creates immediate curiosity about the topic
- Promises a specific, valuable outcome
- Uses conversational, energetic tone suitable for YouTube
- Includes 1-2 power words for emotional impact

**Example output format:**
"Stop wasting hours on [X]. In this video, I'll show you [specific result] in under [time]..."

---

## 2. Video Outline/Script

**Input:** [Video title, target keyword, video length, audience level]

**Prompt:**
Create a detailed video outline/script with:
- 3-5 main sections with clear headings
- Intro hook (same as above)
- Body points with sub-bullets
- Transition phrases between sections
- Key points to emphasize (bold)
- Suggested B-roll/timestamp cues
- Closing summary + CTA

Structure as: **[Timestamp] | Section Title | Key Points | Delivery Notes**

---

## 3. Thumbnail Idea

**Input:** [Video title, top 3 keywords, competitor thumbnails]

**Prompt:**
Generate 3 thumbnail concepts that:
- Use bold, readable text (minimum 30pt equivalent)
- Include 1 human face with exaggerated emotion
- Feature 1-2 high-contrast colors
- Follow current YouTube aesthetic trends
- Avoid: cluttered backgrounds, tiny text, generic stock photos

For each concept, provide:
- Visual composition description
- Suggested text placement
- Color palette
- Emotion/expression cue

---

## 4. Video Title

**Input:** [Video topic, angle, target keyword, competitors]

**Prompt:**
Generate 10 YouTube title options:
- 6 curiosity-driven titles (how-to, listicles, "mistakes" format)
- 2 emotion-driven titles (fear of missing out, transformation)
- 2 keyword-optimized titles (include [target keyword])
- All under 60 characters
- Numbers where applicable (3, 5, 7, 10)
- Power words: Secret, Hidden, Ultimate, Proven, Simple, Fast

Format as a ranked list with brief rationale for top 3.

---

## 5. Description

**Input:** [Video topic, timestamp key points, links to include, social handles]

**Prompt:**
Write a YouTube description with:
- 150-character hook (include main keyword)
- 3-4 sentence value proposition
- Timestamps (format: [00:00] - Section Title)
- Relevant links (playlist, related videos)
- Keywords naturally integrated (avoid keyword stuffing)
- CTA to subscribe at bottom

Include sections: **📌 Hook | 💡 What You'll Learn | 🔗 Links | 👋 Connect**

---

## 6. Community Post

**Input:** [Video topic, audience question, engagement goal]

**Prompt:**
Create a Community Post that:
- Asks a polarizing question related to [video topic]
- Includes 1-2 emoji max
- Is short enough to read in 10 seconds
- Invites personal experience answers
- Option to include: poll, meme, or behind-the-scenes still

**Format options:**
- Question + "Drop your answers below 👇"
- Poll with 2-4 options
- "I asked X about [topic] and here's what they said..."

---

## 7. Shorts Idea

**Input:** [Main video topic, extractable moment, trend format]

**Prompt:**
Generate 5 Shorts concepts from [topic]:
- Each as a standalone hook (under 60 seconds)
- Use "POV," "Wait for it," "Plot twist," or "Hack" formats
- Include 1 visual surprise or reveal
- End with "Watch full video" CTA
- Extract: surprising fact, quick tip, myth-bust, or transformation

Format: **Title | Hook Line | Key Visual | Duration**

---

## 8. Playlist Idea

**Input:** [Channel niche, content pillars, existing videos]

**Prompt:**
Design 3 playlist concepts:
1. **Cornerstone** - Ultimate guide/beginner series
2. **Trending** - Reaction/analysis/updates
3. **Evergreen** - Tips, tutorials, how-tos

For each playlist:
- Catchy title (3-6 words)
- 8-15 video count goal
- Description (50 words max)
- Suggested video order (lead with highest retention)

Include 3 video title suggestions per playlist.

---

## 9. Call-to-Action

**Input:** [Video value delivered, audience, subscribe goal]

**Prompt:**
Create CTAs for:
- **Mid-roll** (retention spike moment): Quick reminder to like + save
- **End screen**: Full CTA with value-reinforcement
- **Soft CTA**: Non-interrupting verbal cue during video

Each CTA should:
- State the action clearly (like, comment, subscribe, share)
- Include 1 reason "because [specific benefit]"
- Use "You" language, not "you guys"
- Feel natural, not salesy

**Tone options:** Grateful, Direct, Conversational, Community-focused
